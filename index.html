How It Works

Make the group decision before the group chat burns out.

Trip Match turns scattered opinions into a shared direction. Everyone swipes, the app finds the overlap, and your travel plan gets clearer with each round.

Inside a trip room

Plan the trip everyone actually wants.

Destinations, dates, and budget guardrails stay visible so the whole group can keep moving without anyone becoming the spreadsheet owner.

  • Shared shortlist, visible momentum
  • Quick yes or no decisions with context
  • Votes that turn into a bookable plan
  1. 01

    Create a trip room

    Start a private room for a couples escape, a birthday trip, or a full crew weekend.

  2. 02

    Invite your partner or group

    Share a simple invite so everyone joins the same planning space in seconds.

  3. 03

    Swipe destinations together

    React to cities, beaches, road trips, and long-haul escapes without endless back-and-forth.

  4. 04

    Match on a destination

    See where the group naturally overlaps and move the best options to the top.

  5. 05

    Vote on dates, flights, and hotel options

    Keep the group aligned on timing and travel tradeoffs without leaving the app flow.

  6. 06

    Book externally and build the itinerary

    Hand off to trusted partners once the match is clear, then keep the plan organized together.
